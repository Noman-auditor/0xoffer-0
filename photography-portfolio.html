About Maya

Every frame tells a story worth remembering

I'm Maya Johnson — a New York-based photographer with over 8 years of experience capturing life's most precious moments. From intimate portraits to grand wedding ceremonies, I bring artistic vision and emotional intelligence to every shoot.

My approach is simple: I listen first, then I shoot. Understanding your vision, your personality, and the story you want to tell allows me to create images that are authentically, beautifully yours.

  • Canon EOS R5 & Sony A7R V — Professional gear
  • Full editing suite — Lightroom, Photoshop, Capture One
  • 48-hour turnaround on event galleries
  • Worldwide travel available for destination shoots
